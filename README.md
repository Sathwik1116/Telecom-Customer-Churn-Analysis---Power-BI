# Telco Customer Churn Analysis (Power BI)

##  Project Overview
Customer churn is a critical challenge in the telecom industry.  
This project analyzes customer churn patterns using Power BI to identify key drivers of churn and assess their revenue impact.

The analysis focuses on customer demographics, service usage, contract types, billing behavior, and churn concentration.

---

##  Objectives
- Identify customer segments with high churn risk
- Analyze the impact of tenure, contracts, and services on churn
- Quantify revenue loss due to customer churn
- Provide data-driven insights to support retention strategies

---

##  Dataset
- Source: Telco Customer Churn Dataset
- Records: 7,043 customers
- Features include:
  - Demographics (gender, senior citizen, dependents)
  - Service subscriptions (internet, add-ons)
  - Billing & contract details
  - Churn status (Yes/No)

---

##  Dashboards

#### Customer Overview & Churn Summary
- Overall churn rate and customer distribution
- Churn analysis by tenure and senior citizen status

#### Service Usage Impact on Churn
- Internet service and add-on impact
- Relationship between number of services and churn

#### Contract & Billing Analysis
- Churn by contract type and payment method
- Monthly vs total charges analysis
- Paperless billing impact

#### Churn Drivers & Revenue Impact
- High-risk churn segments
- Churn concentration by tenure and contract
- Revenue loss due to churn
